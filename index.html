Food Fall

This project will be the simplest one. I made this just to relax and trying to memorize things I had learn from my coding course. It's about catching healthy foods that was falling and let the unhealthy ones fall. Thats why the title is Food Fall. Oh ya, one more, if we catch a water bottle, it will add the health points. It was pretty easy to make since it just took 3 days for me to finish and only consist of few codes & layouts. I just have to find or draw many foods or drinks to be the sprites. This game can be played both on computer and phone but it's easier to play on phone rather than on a computer.
